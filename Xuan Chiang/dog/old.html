Featured Dogs

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is a friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited dog breed.

Average Age: 10-12 years

Height: 21-24 inches

Weight: 55-80 pounds

Labradors excel as family pets, service dogs, and working dogs due to their versatile nature and trainable temperament.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a courageous, confident, and highly intelligent dog breed.

Average Age: 10-13 years

Height: 22-26 inches

Weight: 50-90 pounds

German Shepherds are commonly used in police and military roles, as well as in search and rescue operations.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a friendly, tolerant, and intelligent dog breed.

Average Age: 10-12 years

Height: 21-24 inches

Weight: 55-75 pounds

Golden Retrievers are popular as family pets, therapy dogs, and in various dog sports due to their friendly and adaptable nature.
