What are the coalition?

Created on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

The Coalition comprised of the South Vietnamese, the United States of America, South Korea, Australia, New Zealand, Taiwan, Thailand and the Phillippines. They were against the North and was a anti-communist faction. But sadly, America left the coalition leading to downfall of the Vietnam War. After that, in 1975, South Vietnam fell under the rule of the Communist Party of Vietnam
