Corgi QUIZ

Originally from the british isles, the corgi is a majestic breed of dog with a curvy tail, long, upright ears, a sausage-like body and four drumsick sized legs.

People in wales bred these small but tough dogs to herd sheep.So that shepherds could distinguish corgis from wolves, corgis often had thier tails clipped, resulting in the short, stubby pom-poms so common in corgis today.

the word "Corgi", is derived from the welsh language,with cor meaning dwarf and gi meaning dog.

There are two separate breeds of corgis, pembroke welsh corgis and cardigan welsh corgis, overall, cardigans are larger both in weight and height, the tails are different shapes, with the pembroke's tail drooping as apossed to the cardigan's curly one.

10 interesting facts about corgis are,:

1. For their size, they are suprisingly energetic dogs,and you will need to take them out for at least an hour to satisfy their needs.

2. Corgis shed enough very year to start a cashmere company!

3. Corgis are the shortest breed of herding dog.

4. Some corgis are known to zoom around the house for no apparent reason.

5. The first recorded lineage of Corgis goes as far back as 1107 AD!

6. Corgis have a double coat.

7. The Corgi's common ancestor is the swedish valhund.

8. People in wales associate Corgis with folklore, welsh legend says that fairies and elves used the welsh corgi to work fairy cattle, pull fairy chariots and even serve as a steed for fairy warriors, even today you can see the marks of the "fairy saddle" in the shoulders of the pembroke corgi's coat.

9. Corgis can give birth to up to eight puppies!

10. A pembroke welsh Corgi named Rufus was amazon's mascot!
